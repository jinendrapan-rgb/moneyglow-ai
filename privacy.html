Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026 · Effective immediately

🔒 Your financial data never leaves your device. MoneyGlow AI stores all your income, expenses, and debt data locally in your browser — not on our servers.

1. Information We Collect

MoneyGlow AI is designed with privacy first. Here is what we collect and when:

  • Financial data (income, expenses, debts): Stored only on your device via browser localStorage. Never sent to our servers.
  • Account information (name, email): Optionally provided during signup. If Google Sheets integration is enabled, this is sent to your own Google Sheet — not to us.
  • Referral codes: Generated locally and stored on your device.
  • Usage analytics: We do not use any third-party analytics or tracking scripts.

2. How We Use Your Information

Any information you provide is used solely to:

  • Provide and improve the App's functionality
  • Send your data to your own Google Sheet (only if you configure this feature)
  • Process premium license purchases via our payment processor (Paddle)

3. Google Sheets Integration

If you choose to connect Google Sheets, your data (income, expenses, debts, signup details) is sent to a Google Apps Script you deploy in your own Google account. This data goes directly to your Google Sheet — MoneyGlow AI does not have access to it.

4. Payment Processing

Premium purchases are processed by Paddle (paddle.com). When you make a purchase, you interact directly with Paddle's secure checkout. MoneyGlow AI does not store your payment card details. Please review Paddle's Privacy Policy for details on how they handle payment data.

5. Cookies and Local Storage

MoneyGlow AI uses browser localStorage to save your data and preferences. This data stays on your device and is not accessible to us. We do not use tracking cookies.

6. Data Security

Since all financial data is stored locally on your device, security depends on your device's own security measures. We recommend enabling device lock/PIN and using the App's built-in PIN lock feature (Premium).

7. Children's Privacy

MoneyGlow AI is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Your Rights

You can delete all your data at any time by clearing your browser's localStorage for this site, or using the "Reset" option within the App. Since we don't store your financial data, there is nothing to request deletion of on our end.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page.

10. Contact

For privacy questions or concerns, contact us at hello.moneyglowai@gmail.com.
